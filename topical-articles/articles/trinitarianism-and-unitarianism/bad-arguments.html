Bad Arguments for the Trinity and Deity of Christ

Is Jesus God? - Gotquestions.org

The most important reason that Jesus must be God is that, if He is not God, His death would not have been sufficient to pay the penalty for the sins of the world (1 John 2:2). A created being could not pay the infinite penalty required to forgive sin against an infinite God. But Jesus is an uncreated being; as God, He could pay an infinite penalty.
